What Are SNARKs?

  • A novel and unstested cryptographic technique
  • A system for generating ZK proofs from C programs using a combo of cryptographic and compiler tech
  • Prove to a second party that you know secrets to satisfy a program without revealing the secrets

What Are SNARKs for?

  • Sidechains-> Prove your alt coin is unspendable to get a Bitcoin -Adam Bash et al
  • ZeroCash-> Prove you have a coin to spend without revealing your wallet address
